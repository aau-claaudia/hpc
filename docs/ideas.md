# Share your ideas

This page shows ideas from our GitHub Ideas board.
Use it to suggest new applications, features, or other improvements you would like to see on UCloud.

!!! tip "How to contribute"
    - **Post your idea below** on this page.
        - If you prefer not to post here, submit it via the [Service Portal](https://serviceportal.aau.dk/serviceportal?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=a05e2fb4c3434610f0f3041ad001310e).
    - **Upvote ideas with thumbs up** that you find valuable.
        - Upvotes help us understand community needs and prioritise the roadmap.

!!! info "What to include in your idea"
    Please add a **short summary** of your idea and explain its **purpose**:

    - What should be built or improved?
    - Who would benefit from it?
    - What problem would it solve?
